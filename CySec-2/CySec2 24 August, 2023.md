# Information Security Terminology
- Asset
	- Item of value
- Threat
	- Actions or events that have potential to cause harm
- Threat Agent
	- Person/Element with the power to carry out a threat
		- Can also be natrual disasters
- Asset Types
	- Information
	- Customized Business Software
	- System Software
	- Physical Items
	- Services
- Vulnerability
- Threat Likelihood
- Risk
	- Dealing with Risk
